Key Findings

  • NV-Embed-v2 (8B, 4096-dim) is the new top performer on house_test (RP@1 = .495) and a close second on tech_test
  • ConTeXT-ST (domain-specific, 768-dim, open-source) leads on tech_test (RP@1 = .602) — 40× smaller than NV-Embed-v2
  • Instruction-tuned LLM embeddings (NV-Embed-v2) benefit from task-specific query prompts
  • Fine-tuning voyage-4-nano on synthetic ESCO data narrows the gap to leading models
  • Label-only embedding consistently beats label+context for non-native contextual models
  • Model size ≠ accuracy: MiniLM (22M params) outperforms voyage-4-large (API, 1024d) and KaLM-Gemma3-12B (12B params, 3840-dim)

Recommendations

Best Raw Accuracy

NV-Embed-v2 — 8B LLM embeddings, highest house RP@1 (.495) and RP@10 (.917 tech). Requires GPU.

Best Efficiency Trade-off

ConTeXT-ST — 768-dim, open-source, 40× smaller, highest tech RP@1 (.602). Ideal for production.

Best Fine-tuned Open-Source

voyage-4-nano-ft — fine-tuned on ESCO, local inference, competitive performance.

Strategy

Use label-only embedding. Reserve contextual strategies for native contextual models only.
